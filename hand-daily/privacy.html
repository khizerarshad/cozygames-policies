Hand Daily: Roguelite Edition: Privacy Policy

App: Hand Daily (package com.cozygames.handdaily)
Version covered: 1.0 and forward, until this policy is amended.
Effective date: 2026-06-11.
Last updated: 2026-06-11.

What we collect

Hand Daily collects a small amount of data to make the game work across devices and to track basic engagement. Nothing collected identifies you personally.

  • Anonymous user ID. A randomly generated string created on first launch and stored on your device. Used to sync your unlock progress between devices if you sign in to Google Play Games.
  • Gameplay events. Aggregate events like "ante completed", "joker purchased", "run ended". Sent to Google's Firebase Analytics so we can see which features players use. Each event is bound to the anonymous user ID, never to your name or email.
  • Crash reports. If the game crashes, a crash report goes to Google's crash-reporting service so we can fix the bug. The report includes the device model, Android version, and the line of code that failed.
  • Build identifier. The version of the app you're running. Used to filter analytics by release.

What we do NOT collect

We do not collect:

  • Your name, email, or phone number.
  • Your location, GPS or IP-derived.
  • Your contacts.
  • Your photos, videos, or files.
  • Your microphone, camera, or audio.
  • Your calendar.
  • Any payment information. Hand Daily v1.0 has no in-app purchases.
  • Any browsing data outside the app.

Where the data goes

The data leaves your device only to reach these services:

  • Google Firebase Analytics. Receives the gameplay events listed above. Bound to the anonymous user ID. Firebase privacy policy.
  • Google Play Games Services. Stores your cloud save (a single blob of unlock progress, achievement state, and run history) and your leaderboard scores if you sign in. Sign-in is optional. Without it, the game runs entirely on local storage. Play Games privacy.
  • Google Firebase Crashlytics. Receives crash reports. Firebase privacy policy.

All data in transit is encrypted via TLS.

Ads

Hand Daily v1.0 ships with no ads. No advertising SDK is active in this release. A future release may add interstitial or rewarded ads via Google AdMob; this policy will be updated when that happens, and the Play Store data-safety form will be re-declared.

Children

Hand Daily targets ages 13 and up. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has been collected, contact us at the address below and we'll delete any associated records.

Deleting your data

You can remove your data in two ways:

  • Uninstall. Removes all local save data, including the anonymous user ID.
  • Play Games data deletion. Open the Google Play Games app, find Hand Daily, and choose "Delete game data". This removes the cloud save from Google's servers.

To request deletion of any analytics records linked to your anonymous user ID, email us at the address below with the user ID visible in Settings > About in the game.

Changes to this policy

If we change what we collect or where it goes, the "Last updated" date at the top of this page changes too. Significant changes will be flagged inside the app on next launch.
