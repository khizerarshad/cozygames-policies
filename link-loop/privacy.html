Link Loop: Privacy Policy

App: Link Loop (package com.cozygames.linkloop)
Version covered: 1.0 and forward, until this policy is amended.
Effective date: 2026-06-25.
Last updated: 2026-06-25.

Link Loop is a connect-the-dots puzzle game published by Cozygames. It has no account, no login, and no in-app purchases. The small amount of data it handles is for showing ads and understanding which features players use. Nothing collected identifies you personally.

What we collect

  • Advertising ID. Google's resettable advertising identifier, used by the ad SDK (Google AdMob) to show ads and limit how often you see the same one. It is not linked to your name or email.
  • Gameplay events. Aggregate events like "level completed" and which mode you played, sent to Google Firebase Analytics so we can see which features players use. Bound to an anonymous identifier, never to your name or email.
  • Approximate, non-precise signals used by ads. The ad SDK may use coarse, IP-derived region and device information (device model, Android version, language) to serve and measure ads. We do not collect precise GPS location.
  • Build identifier. The version of the app you're running, used to filter analytics by release.

What we do NOT collect

  • Your name, email, or phone number.
  • Your precise location (no GPS).
  • Your contacts, photos, videos, or files.
  • Your microphone, camera, or audio.
  • Any payment information — Link Loop has no in-app purchases and no subscriptions.
  • Any browsing data outside the app.

Ads

Link Loop is free and supported by ads through Google AdMob. It shows banner, interstitial (between some levels), and rewarded ads (which you choose to watch in exchange for a hint or extra moves). Ads use the advertising ID described above.

  • Consent (EEA, UK, and similar regions). On first launch we show Google's consent form (UMP). Your choice decides whether you see personalized or non-personalized ads.
  • You can reset or limit ad tracking any time in your device settings under Settings → Google → Ads (reset or delete the advertising ID, or opt out of ad personalization).
  • See the Google Ads privacy & terms and AdMob documentation for details on how Google uses this data.

Where the data goes

Data leaves your device only to reach these Google services, over an encrypted (TLS) connection:

  • Google AdMob — serves and measures ads. AdMob policy.
  • Google Firebase Analytics — receives the aggregate gameplay events. Firebase privacy.

Your level progress, coins, and daily-streak save data stay on your device (local storage). On the web versions (YouTube Playables / Playgama) that save is handled by the platform you're playing on, per that platform's policy.

Children

Link Loop is rated for everyone but is intended for a general audience aged 13 and up. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided data, contact us at the address below and we'll delete any associated records.

Deleting your data

  • Uninstall removes all local save data from your device.
  • Reset your advertising ID in Settings → Google → Ads to break the link the ad SDK uses.
  • To request deletion of any analytics records, email us at the address below.

Changes to this policy

If we change what we collect or where it goes, the "Last updated" date at the top of this page changes too. Significant changes will be flagged inside the app on next launch.
